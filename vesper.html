Noir Vesper

ノワール・ヴェスパー

It's his childish nature that allows him to speak so freely about his dreams. He might even actually let everyone around him dream.

Adventurer's Guild TEMPUS's academic advisor. He also holds the posts of custodian and arch-scholar at the Grand Library.

Logical and rational at all times, he doesn't lift a finger unless completely convinced by something. He has an aversion to sunlight due to spending too long cooped up, absorbed in his research.
